30 oktober 2007

Samvirkelaget 14.8.2007






No updates in three months.. Not good, so I decided to put up some photos I took in the beginning of this semester. I had them up in Photoshop anyways. The photos are of the Norwegian ska/hip-hop band Samvirkelaget.

Else I can mention that the semester up until now has been quite hectic with some political trips, practical weeks making radio, attending a wedding etc. Now things are not so hectic, but it's all about a big paper due for the end of November. Exams for this semester are mid December.

Next semester I'm going to be part of the big ugly media industry as a trainee for two months. I will be working for NRK (the Norwegian Broadcasting Corporation) in national news production (Dagsnytt), making news for radio and the internet.
